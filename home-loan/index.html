Required documents

Exact documents can change by lender, loan type and applicant profile. Keep the following ready for discussion:

  • Aadhaar Card, PAN Card and photographs
  • Income proof, salary slips or ITR where applicable
  • Bank statements
  • Property papers, map or estimate as per case
  • Employment, business or professional proof

Eligibility

Eligibility is assessed by the bank or NBFC. These points are commonly reviewed before a lender takes a final decision:

  • Applicant should have steady income
  • Property should meet lender documentation standards
  • Credit profile and repayment capacity should match policy
  • Age, tenure and co-applicant details are reviewed by lender
