Approval se pehle kya check hota hai?

Lenders income, job stability, CIBIL score, existing EMI aur bank statement dekhte hain. Ayodhya ya Faizabad mein apply karte time documents clear hone chahiye.

Practical tips

Keep documents ready

Aadhaar, PAN, salary slip ya income proof, bank statement aur photo ready rakhein.

CIBIL score ko ignore na karein

Late payment aur high credit card usage approval ko weak kar sakta hai.

Loan amount realistic rakhein

EMI income ke hisaab se comfortable honi chahiye. Over-borrowing se rejection risk badhta hai.
