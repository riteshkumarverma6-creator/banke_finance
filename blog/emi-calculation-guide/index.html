EMI kya hoti hai?

EMI yani Equated Monthly Installment. Ye woh fixed monthly payment hai jo borrower loan repayment ke liye karta hai.

EMI kin cheezon par depend karti hai?

Loan amount

Amount zyada hoga to EMI bhi zyada hogi.

Interest rate

Rate kam hone se EMI aur total interest dono par impact hota hai.

Tenure

Long tenure EMI kam kar sakta hai, lekin total interest badh sakta hai.
