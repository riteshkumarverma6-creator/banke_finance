Home loan process kya hota hai?

Home Loan in Ayodhya ke liye pehle income, age, CIBIL score, existing EMI aur property documents check kiye jaate hain. Aaru Finance Solution customers ko ye samjhata hai ki kaunsa document ready rakhna hai aur lender process kaise chalega.

Step-by-step application guide

1. Requirement clear karein

Loan amount, property location, down payment aur EMI comfort decide karein. Ayodhya, Faizabad aur Deokali area ki property ke papers alag-alag case ke hisaab se check hote hain.

2. Documents ready karein

Aadhaar, PAN, income proof, bank statement, photo aur property papers basic requirement hote hain. Self-employed customers ko business proof bhi lag sakta hai.

3. Eligibility aur EMI samjhein

EMI comfortable honi chahiye. Sirf low interest dekhna enough nahi hota; tenure, processing charges aur repayment plan bhi samajhna zaroori hai.
