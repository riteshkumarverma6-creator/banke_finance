Common rejection reasons

Low CIBIL score

Late payments, defaults ya high credit usage loan profile ko weak kar sakte hain.

Incomplete documents

Aadhaar, PAN, bank statement, income proof ya property paper missing hone se process delay ya reject ho sakta hai.

High existing EMI

Agar current EMI burden zyada hai, lender repayment risk dekh sakta hai.

Income mismatch

Application, bank statement aur income proof details match honi chahiye.
