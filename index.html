Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common loan queries

How long does approval take?

Typically 3-7 business days after document submission, depending on lender review and verification timelines.

Can I apply with a low credit score?

Yes, we work with partner lenders who may consider applications with moderate scores based on income and document strength.

Do I need property documents for all loans?

If you apply for property-related or LAP loans, relevant property papers are required. For bike and mobile finance, ID and income proofs are enough.

Are there hidden charges?

We prioritize transparency. All costs are shared upfront and any lender fees are discussed before you proceed.
