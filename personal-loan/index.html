Required documents

Exact documents can change by lender, loan type and applicant profile. Keep the following ready for discussion:

Eligibility

Eligibility is assessed by the bank or NBFC. These points are commonly reviewed before a lender takes a final decision:

  • Indian resident with valid KYC documents
  • Stable income from salary, business or profession
  • Bank statement showing repayment capacity
  • Credit profile and existing EMI obligations acceptable to lender policy
